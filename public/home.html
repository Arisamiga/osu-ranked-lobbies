Frequently Asked Questions

How do the ranks work?

The ranks are calculated based on how well you do against other players in the lobby. A player getting the first place in every game will rank up very fast. There is no difference between getting 1 and 10000000 more points than the next in line.

Will mods make me rank up faster?

No. Mods are mostly preference. They affect your score according to vanilla osu! rules, so it might give you an edge, but consistency is key. Playing HDHR is more of a flex than anything. Looking at you, the 4 digits stuck in Plat.

How are the maps chosen?

They're chosen based on the average skill of the players in the lobby. To be more specific, the bot gets a "comfortable pp level" for each player based on their top 100 plays, and then gets the median value for the lobby. From then on, it simply chooses a map close to that pp level.

Why choose maps from pp instead of stars?

A level's star rating is a good indicator of whether or not you can finish the map. A level's pp rating is a good indicator of whether or not you can FC the map. There's not much point in competing on reading skills - you already know if you can or can't pass a certain star level. Competing on consistency, though? Now we're talking.

What's the map pool?

The map pool is all maps that have a leaderboard: ranked, approved and loved maps. So, about 90k maps total.

Do you use Elo for the rating algorithm?

Nope, it's Elo-MMR, which is better suited to multiplayer games. You can read the information given in that link for more details.
